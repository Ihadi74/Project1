About Me

I am a trilingual international professional with a strong background in Continuous Improvement, Business Process Management, and Project Management. My passion lies in optimizing processes, increasing efficiency, reducing costs, and enhancing customer satisfaction—a mindset I now bring into the world of Full-Stack Development.

Currently enrolled in a Full-Stack Development program, I see this not as a complete shift but as a natural extension of my expertise.

I am an agile learner who thrives in dynamic and demanding environments. My ability to adapt, innovate, and lead cross-functional teams allows me to bridge the gap between business needs and technology. I hold an MBA from Durham Business School (UK), a Bachelor's in Computer Science from the University of Quebec in Montreal (UQAM), and PMP and Lean Six Sigma Black Belt Certifications.

As a leader and mentor, I enjoy helping others, sharing knowledge, and simplifying complex processes to empower teams and students. My projects focus on providing sustainable solutions that enhance productivity and efficiency.
